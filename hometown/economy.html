Economy

Bengaluru's economy thrives on fostering innovation and entrepreneurship

OVERVIEW

According to the Times of India census 2022, there are about 1.5 million workers employed in the IT and IT related services. Apart from IT companies, Bangalore is also home to a number of businessess including small businesses and some multi million businesses. A number of people come to Bangalore to setup their own business in the city and earn a living. As the city boosts of a large population, it makes it ideal for all kinds of business. There are a wide variety of businesses found in the city ranging from manufacturing cotton, processing wood, recycling of plastic bottles etc.
