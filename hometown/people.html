Faces of Bengaluru

Bengaluru is a melting pot of cultures and stories waiting to be discovered

Tech wizards

The fast paced world of Bengaluru, from bustling tech parks to the focused atmosphere of co-working spaces.

Street Food Maestros

From the sizzle of masala dosas to the aroma of filter coffee, Bengaluru's vibrant street food culture is crafted by local street vendors.

Artists and Performers

Bengaluru's thriving artists and performers contribute to the city's energy of street art.

Students

Students in Bengaluru contribute significantly to the city's modern and diverse atmosphere. Bengaluru, being a hub for education and tech, attracts a vast student population from across the country and even internationally.

Cultural and Religious communities

Bengaluru is home to people from various cultural and religious backgrounds. It has places of worship for different religions and hosts festivals that bring together people from diverse communities.

Entrepreneurs and Business owners

Bengaluru has a thriving startup culture, and the city is home to many entrepreneurs and innovators. The startup ecosystem has contributed to the city's reputation as the startup capital of India.
